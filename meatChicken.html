About our meat chicken

Our chickens are brown feathered, free-range, minimum 14-weeks old. The average dressed weight is 2.5-3lbs. They are fed with Non-GMO chicken feed with no chemicals or hormonal substances. The chickens are processed at an Ontario inspected and regulated processing plant. They will be plucked and cleaned with no head and no feet.
